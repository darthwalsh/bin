## TODO snippets from disk
Raycast's built-in snippet feature is designed for manually created or imported content (via JSON file). It does not natively support reading snippet content directly from a file on your disk. 

However, two methods allow you to use file content for snippets:

- **Use a third-party extension:** The [Snippet Surfer extension](https://www.raycast.com/astronight/snippetsurfer) for Raycast is specifically designed to read snippets from a folder of Markdown or YAML files on your disk.
