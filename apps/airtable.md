# How to Merge Links to Other Record
By default, copy-paste in airtable will **overwrite** the target record.

**Airtable linked-record merge workaround**
To merge linked-record entries manually:
1. Copy the source linked-record cell from Airtable.
2. Paste it into a plaintext editor.
	1. Airtable turns it into a comma-separated list of record names/IDs.
3. Combine multiple copied cells by editing that comma-separated text.
4. Copy the combined text.
5. Paste it back into the target linked-record cell to merge the links.
