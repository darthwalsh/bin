## Same-name foreign units

These are units that share a name with a US unit but normally differ:

| Foreign                              |                   USA |
| ------------------------------------ | --------------------: |
| UK/CA pound (lb)                     |           1.000 US lb |
| UK long hundredweight (cwt = 112 lb) |           1.12 US cwt |
| Metric ton / tonne (1000 kg)         | 1.102 US (short) tons |
| UK long ton (2240 lb)                |  1.12 US (short) tons |
| UK/CA fluid ounce (fl oz)            |        0.961 US fl oz |
| Metric cup (250 mL)                  |         1.057 US cups |
| UK/CA pint                           |        1.201 US pints |
| UK/CA quart                          |       1.201 US quarts |
| UK/CA gallon                         |      1.201 US gallons |
| Metric horsepower (PS)               |   0.986 mechanical hp |

> [!NOTE] Imperial is used in UK/CA (sometimes incorrectly labeled as "Imperial" in the USA).
> but pound is identical by 1959 treaty
